Finish the Jam

A 2D character animated inside a 3D environment


Tools Used: Blender

About the Project

The company Daily Ops was looking for a way to reduce irritation and road rage in traffic jams. They wanted an application that people could use to socialise with others stuck in traffic jams. ​ To achieve this the application had to obey to the traffic laws in regard to mobile phone usage. As a result the app was designed to be able to be controlled hands free by the usage of a maximum of 4 motion controls, to make use of a speech guide and to be easy to read in a quick glance. ​ The app is based off a game called "finish the lyric" where the player has to continue singing the song after it stops playing.
